Good to Know

Questions

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by June 25 so we can get an accurate headcount.

Can I bring a plus one?

If your invitation says "and guest," then yes. If not, we would prefer to celebrate just you due to venue restrictions.

Can I bring my children?

Unless your children are listed on your invite when you RSVP, this is an adults-only event.

Can I take pictures?

Yes — but please avoid taking pictures during the church ceremony. After the ceremony, you can take as many as you'd like and upload them through our gallery.
